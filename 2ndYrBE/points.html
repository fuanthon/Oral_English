Points and Grading

Breakdown of Final Grade:

50% = Final Exam
50% = Points

where:
900 points = 70%
1000 points = 80%
1100 points = 90%
1200 points = 100%

The grade that you can obtain from the points is a step function. This means that, for example, you must get at least 900 points to get a 70%. Then to get 80%, you must reach 1000 points, and when you have between 900 and 1000 points, your grade is not affected.


How to earn points:

Turning in written work from class activity = calculated out of 50 points
Turn in homework:

  • C rank homework = 10 points
  • B rank homework = 30 points
  • A rank homework = 40 points
  • S rank homework = 70 points
